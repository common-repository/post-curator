Get zero effort do follow backlinks to your blog posts with Post Curator. Add this plugin and we will automatically link to your content for free.

== Description ==
Post Curator is a do follow link directory. Fully automatic, it will periodically check your blog for new posts and link to them. How Post Curator works.

1.  Go to Settings > Post Curator.
2.  Choose the most appropriate category for your blog.
3.  Click the button to register your blog with our servers.
4.  Click Save.

To display the link to your blog we take the Title of the post, a 250 character preview from the content, the URL for the featured image and any tags. We do not show the content of the post, just enough info to create a decent listing and entice some clicks for you. We will add new links to your content at a rate of about 1 per day.

Automated link submissions to Post Curator are free.

== Installation ==
Download from the Plugin Installer within WordPress. Install and Activate.

Alternatively extract the zip file and just drop the contents in the wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation and then activate the Plugin from Plugins page.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

Do you take my content?  
No, we link directly to your content from the listing pages.

Are the links do follow?  
Yes

Link keywords?  
We use the title of the post as the link text.

Where are my links displayed?
Your posts will be linked from <http://postcurator.net>
